Emilia Lindholm is an interaction designer with a great interest in, and love for, games. She is strongest in illustration, but also knows how to put together websites, and how to prototype apps and games. For example, she put together this website! With the help of tutorials and friends, teamwork makes the dream work. ;9

Currently based in Sweden, she grew up in Luxembourg, with frequent visits to her homeland Finland, where she also studied Comic Art and Graphic Design for a year. She also briefly worked in Denmark and was an exchange student in South Korea. She speaks Swedish and English fluently, German and French a bit rusty-ly and speaks basic Japanese. (She can also read the Korean alphabet and order food from menus, but doesn't have a big enough vocabulary to actually speak much, oops!)
